This is the data repository for "The Impact of Systems Engineering and Process Optimization Strategies on Patient Experience and Outcome Disparities for Limited English Proficiency Populations: An Ethnographic Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis", a study conducted under ThinkNeuro, LLC's Second Medical Language Exchange Internship.

- Scripts to generate all figures and statistical analyses are included in several formats.
- Raw unedited figures generated by the code are also provided; these were edited in Google Drawings for clarity and to remove any error.
- The original study data uploaded into RStudio is "Meta-analysis data (3).xlsx" and is referenced in the scripts.
- The data collection template forms were compiled into one sheet and included in this repository.
- Some internal scripts to display and clarify metadata, and to display risk of bias as a traffic light diagram for subgroup analyses, are also included and were used in data stratification and in final quality-of-evidence assessments.
